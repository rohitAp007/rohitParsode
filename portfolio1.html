CyberAware App

Cybersecurity awareness is crucial in daily activities, such as web browsing, email checking, and online interactions. It is the responsibility of business leaders to emphasize its importance to all employees. While not everyone needs in-depth technical knowledge, role-based training helps staff understand relevant cybersecurity threats. Tailored training for different teams ensures effective cybersecurity awareness, fostering lasting behavior change. A specialized application acts as a mediator, educating those unfamiliar with cybersecurity on both the benefits and risks of digital tools like mobile phones and online transactions. The app features modules including login, actions, IPC sections by crime, recent cybercrime case studies, and protocols, enhancing cybersecurity awareness among users.
